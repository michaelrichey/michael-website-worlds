THE

SAMURAI-PIZZA-CATS

FAN SITE

Welcome to the Samurai-Pizza-Cats Fan Site—a place for people who are fans of samurai, pizza, and cats. To join our group, you can't love just two of these things, say samurai and cats, you must love all three. For us, the love of these three things brings perfect balance to our fandom.

Side note: if you came here hoping to find fans of Samurai Pizza Cats, Saban's 1993 English localization of the Tatsunoko anime Kyatto Ninden Teyandee, then I'm afraid you've found this site in error. I suggest you turn around and hit that back button. That is... unless you happen to be a fan of Samurai Pizza Cats and samurai, pizza, and cats! That would be unlikely though... We've yet to meet anyone who can maintain such a fandom balance.
