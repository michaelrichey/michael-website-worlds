A List of Things Sonic Sez No To

As you well know, Sonic is famous for waving and wagging his index finger. He's done this from his very first game on the Sega Genesis (MegaDrive in Europe and Japan) up until the present day.

But why does the much fan arted hedgehod wave his digit thusly? It's not to be cheeky. In fact, it's because he's telling us all "no." He is making it clear that he does not want what you're selling and will not agree to join in on whatever scheme you're planning.

Things Sonic Sez No To
Back Braces
Pizza
Laundry Day
Digital TV Antennae
Hardwood Flooring
Carpet
City Life
Campaign Contributions
Eggs
Shows based on movies that have the subtitle "The Animated Series"
Clothing except shoes
Fan fiction of himself
Fan art of himself
Your fan art and fan fiction specifically, Ryan
And many more...

Hopefully this non-exhaustive list will help you better understand what Sonic might say yes to, so you can have an easier time pitching him your ideas. Good luck!
