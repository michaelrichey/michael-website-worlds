The True Story of Lance Crackers

Philip Lance and Salem Van Every, Lance's son-in-lawThe son of a different family that is married to a daughter of another family and by law, or "in-law," becomes part of another family., started selling single serve snacks in 1913. Two years later, they opened the doors of the Lance Packaging Company in North Carolina. In 1943, Philip Lance Van Every took over leadership of Lance, still a privately held company. Today, Lance, Inc., is a leader in the snack food industry and one of the largest manufacturers and distributors of snack foods in the United States, especially in the South.
