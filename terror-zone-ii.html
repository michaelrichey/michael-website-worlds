WELCOME, CHILDREN, TO MY CRYPT OF WOE!

Hello there, wanderers. You have stumbled into my mausoleum of unnatural tales and spooky stories. I am V.L. Smythe, and I have been penning horror chapter books called SHIVERSPINES that have haunted the "pre-teen" section of libraries and Waldenbooks all over the world.

I wouldn't blame you if you turned around and walked out of this website right now. Most do. But if you are in the mood for pages and pages of build up followed by a SHOCKING TWIST or a mild FRIGHT, then enter the world SHIVERSPINES and drink deep the non-alcoholic wine of FEAR!

"SHIVERSPINES" BOOKS

Mouse over or tap the book covers to learn more about the stories... more than you'd want to know, my creepies!

The Creepy Tree

Milly and Sam thought they were going to have normal fall break, until a tree with an ugly face moves into their neighborhood.

The Ghost of Haunted Cove

Sarah and Frankie thought there wouldn't be anything strange at haunted cove. But a ghostly spirit is about to prove that to be untrue.

The Skeleton's Gambling Problem

Pamela and Jiffy thought they would have a relaxing summer vacation. That is until they fall through the roof of an abandoned casino and meet an old skeleton that can't stop rolling them bones.

Vampire for Hire!

Herbert and Doris though they would have a regular Christmas break working at their grandfather's general store. But when a homeless vampire starts hanging around outside the front door, they have to decide whether or not to give him a part-time job.

Werewolf Picnic

Mikey and Abigail thought they had it made when their fourth of July break started. But after chasing a stray volleyball into the woods, they find themselves uninvited guests at a lycan family reunion.

Ding Dong! Zombie's at the Door

Winnie and Harold are excited to stay home alone while their parents go to the dinner. Just then, the doorbell rings. Through peephole is a zombie, and he wants to sell them terror.

Did That Merman Just Wink At Me?

Horace and Beverly are ready for a fun day at the beach. Little do they know a merman lives in a nearby cove, and he has a hankering for closing one eye while leaving the other open.

Lawn Jockeys Thieve Your Supper!

Penny and Richard can't wait for their normal winter snow day. Unfortunately, they left their dinner on the table as they built their snowman, and some hungry lawn jockeys just came to life thanks to a nearby wizard's magic. The wizard has been watching them from a nearby lighthouse, and he's just been waiting for the chance to prank them by animating some kind of statue.

The Empty Graveyard

Walter and Sylvia visit their parents' graves.
