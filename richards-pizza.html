Buona Pizza! Welcome to the homepage of my restaurant, Richard's Pizza Restaurante, located in the Glenmoor Commons Shopping Plaza on HWY 218. When I was a young boy I ate a slice of BELLISIMO pizza while watching the Godfather, and that's when I knew that when I grew up, I wanted to be Italian. Fifteen years later, I bought DiMaggio's Pizza from Papa DiMaggio, changed the name to Richard's Pizza Restaurante, and started my business.

Take a look around the page then come on down and visit us. And bring the family! (No singles.)
