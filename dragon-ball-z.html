CHARACTERS

GOKU

Goku is the leader of the Z Squad and the real heart of the team. He's a Saiyan and sometimes goes Super Saiyan when his friends need his ultimate power. He has a power pole that can extend and a magic cloud he can fly on, but now that he can go Super Saiyan, he doesn't use them anymore—he threw them both in a closet and covered them in forgotten winter coats.

VEGETA

Vegeta is the prince of all Saiyans once again. He came from his planet to kill Goku. He threatened Goku's entire family. But then they fought, and now they're bros.

PICCOLO

Piccolo was an evil demon king from the planet Namek who tried to kill Goku, and he threatened Goku's entire family. Also Piccolo kidnapped Goku's son and left him in the forest. But then they fought, and now they're bros.

SAGAS

Dragon Ball Z is an epic story filled with life and death battles, and this massive story is divided into sagas.

Saiyan Saga

Saiyans come to Earth to kill Goku.


Frieza Saga

Frieza wants the Dragon Balls and comes to Earth. Goku becomes Super Saiyan!


Androids Saga

Some Androids are unleashed. Trunks comes from the future and chops up Frieza.


Cell Saga

Cell the Android starts swallowing people into his tail and gets powerful and starts a tournament.


Buu Saga

A pink demon who loves candy fights everyone. Goku becomes Super Saiyan 3!
